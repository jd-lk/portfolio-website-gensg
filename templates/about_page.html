About Me:
Thanks for dropping by!

In a previous life, I was a copywriter (though I'd sometimes prefer the term "storyteller"), marketer, and product manager. I was a ghostwriter too. It was soul-sucking work (pun intended).

Over time, I have had the pleasure of producing marketable and thought leadership content for both small and large clients alike; sharing their stories, their history, and helping mold their brand.

More than simply crafting copy, my roles as part of greater, collaborative teams have provided me with the appreciation necessary in working towards optimizing SEO, Web Design, UX, as well as the general tomfoolery that comes when thinking of the next tagline that'll knock your socks off or trying to explain why hierarchical URL structures are better than flat ones.

But these days, I'm embracing the inner nerd in me and doing something I've finally wanted to all these years; lose myself in code and systems.

Work aside, I'm an avid video gamer who's a sucker for jaw-dropping story lines. Half of the fun arises from critiquing game design choices such as a lack of player agency, laughing at badly written characters, or dialogue that has about as much personality as a plank of wood. I love reading, philosophy, and while not embroiled in work, I sometimes function as a part-time drummer and DJ.

But enough one-sided chatter. Head on to the Contact page to connect with me! :)
